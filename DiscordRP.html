Discord Rich Presence


See your Satisfactory presence info on your Discord profile.



This mod will show your in-game status on your Discord profile. It currently has support for biome, tier, activity, and elapsed time in the active game session. Note: Currently the mod only works if you play Satisfactory in English. It partially works in other languages but the biome thumbnail will not display.







More info


Compatibility Notes:



Currently the mod does not fully support multiplayer, only the host will be able to get presence info, but it will not work for the client(s).



As the game is updated and new items are added this mod will be updated as well.
