SatisWHACKtory


FICSIT Inc. is not responsible for any injuries caused by the use of this mod.



Tired of building factories all day? Just want to have some fun? Then this mod is for you! It adds 8 new obstacles (With more on the way!) for you to make chaotic obstacle courses or just mess around and have fun. You can get smacked in the helmet with a giant pendulum, knocked over by a spinning bar, or even yeeted out of a cannon at mach 10. By the end of it, you'll be laughing your head off!

As of release the buildings provided by the mod are:



  • 3 Spinning Bar Variations

  • Spinning Hammer

  • Wrecking Ball

  • Yeet Cannon (NOTE: You have to stand on the green box and wait 5 seconds for it to yeet you.)

  • Gas Emitter

  • Kill Floor

All of these buildings can be unlocked in a milestone in HUB tier 1.

I hope you enjoy this mod! If you encounter a bug while using the mod please open an issue ticket on the GitHub page or ping me on the Satisfactory Modding Discord @SpaceGameDev568 (Factory Props), though please refrain from directly messaging (DM'ing) me unless you are disclosing a serious issue such as a security vulnerability.
